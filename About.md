## How to Install This App on Your Device

You can install this resource guide as an app on your phone, tablet, or computer for quick access‚Äîeven when you‚Äôre offline.

### If You See the Install Button

If you see an ‚ÄúInstall App‚Äù button (as shown in the table above) in the bottom-right corner of the screen, just tap it!
The button appears when your browser supports direct installation.

### iPhone or iPad (Safari)

1. Open this page in Safari (the default web browser)
2. Tap the share button (the square with an up arrow) at the bottom of the screen
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add** in the top right corner

*Note: On iPhone/iPad you must use Safari for this feature to work. Other browsers like Chrome or Firefox do not support installing web apps on iOS.*

### Android (Chrome)

1. Tap the menu button (**‚ãÆ**) in the top right corner
2. Tap **Add to Home screen** or **Install app**
3. Tap **Install** to confirm

### Android (Firefox)

1. Tap the menu button (**‚ãÆ**)
2. Tap **Install** or **Add to Home screen**

### Android (Samsung Internet)

### Windows, Mac, or Linux (Chrome, Edge, or Brave)
